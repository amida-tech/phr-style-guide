6.0 - Panels

Panels are used to 'box' information and visually differentiate components.

6.1 - General Panel Formatting

Default styling

Sample Title
Content goes here.
This is a footer

 

<div class="panel panel-default">
 <div class="panel-heading">
   <div class="panel-title">Sample Title</div>
 </div>
 <div class="panel-body">
   Content goes here.
 </div>
 <div class="panel-footer">This is a footer</div>
</div>

6.2 - Paneled Tables

Add visual structure to information with multiple components.

Default styling

Sample panel title
One ValueTwo ValueThree Value
Next ValueNext Two ValueNext Three Value
Panel Footer

 

<div class="panel panel-default">
 <div class="panel-heading">
   <div class="panel-title">Sample panel title</div>
 </div>
   <table class="table">
     <tr><td>One Value</td><td>Two Value</td><td>Three Value</td></tr>
     <tr><td>Next Value</td><td>Next Two Value</td><td>Next Three Value</td></tr>
   </table>
   <div class="panel-footer">Panel Footer</div>
 </div>

6.3 - Collapsable Panels

Collapsing a panel reduces information-overload by hiding components that are less relevant.

Default styling

Panel One
Panel one collapsed content here
Panel Two
Panel two collapsable content

 

<div class="panel-group" id="accordion">
 <div class="panel panel-default">
   <div class="panel-heading">
     <div class="panel-title">
       <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapseOne">
         Panel One
       </a>
     </div>
   </div>
   <div id="collapseOne" class="panel-collapse collapse in">
     <div class="panel-body">
       Panel one collapsed content here
     </div>
   </div>
 </div>
 <div class="panel panel-default">
   <div class="panel-heading">
     <div class="panel-title">
       <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion" href="#collapseTwo">
         Panel Two
       </a>
     </div>
   </div>
   <div id="collapseTwo" class="panel-collapse collapse">
     <div class="panel-body">
       Panel two collapsable content
     </div>
   </div>
 </div>
</div>
