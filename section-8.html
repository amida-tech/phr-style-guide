8.0 - Forms

Covers styles used for forms, such as the <input> and <select> elements in the Registration and Log In process

8.1 - Log In and Registration Form Style

This style will be used for text input fields such as during log in and when a user manually enters information during registration.

Default styling

Help text goes here: Please enter your primary email address.

 

<form role="form">
 <div class="form-group">
   <label for="exampleInputEmail1">Email address</label>
   <input type="email" class="form-control" id="exampleInputEmail1" placeholder="Enter email">
     <span class="help-block">Help text goes here: Please enter your primary email address.</span>
 </div>
 <div class="form-group">
   <label for="exampleInputPassword1">Password</label>
   <input type="password" class="form-control" id="exampleInputPassword1" placeholder="Password">
 </div>
 <button type="submit" class="btn btn-primary">Log In</button>
<button type="cancel" class="btn btn-default">Cancel</button>
</form>
